FAQ

Below you will find the answers to our customers' most frequently asked questions.

Nanny Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your nanny within the first three months, Nannies CA will provide you with a replacement for no additional charge.

How long does will it take for you to find me a nanny?

Nannies CA strives to find the perfect nanny for your family within two weeks. If we currently have a nanny on staff that is not with a family we may be able to provide you with services sooner. If there are special qualifications that you would like your nanny to have it may take us slightly longer to provide you with a nanny.

What is your nanny screening process?

We strive to ensure that the nanny is safe, reliable, and trustworthy. To do this, Nannies CA first conducts a phone interview with several candidates. Next, we conduct in person interviews with the nanny that revolve around situations that they may encounter while interacting with your child / children. We also conduct criminal background checks, check for a valid driver's license, and ensure that each nanny has their CPR certification. We are also able to perform any additional checks at your request.

How does the nanny get paid?

After we find you a nanny that fits your needs you will pay them based upon the agreed upon terms. We will find someone that will work for the rate you want.

Can I meet the nanny you have chosen prior to his/her hiring?

Yes, we can customize our search process to suit your needs. We can also arrange for you to meet with several candidates. We want you to feel 100% comfortable with your family's nanny.

What happens if I am not satisfied with my nanny?

If you are not satisfied with your nanny for any reason within three months from their start date we will find you a replacement free of charge. We will notify you of the three month mark when we decide on a nanny and will also check with you one week before that mark to ensure everything is going well.
