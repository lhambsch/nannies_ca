FAQ

Below you will find the answers to our customers' most frequently asked questions.

Nanny Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your nanny, Nannies CA will provide you with a replacement for no additional charge.

How long does will it take for you to find me a nanny?

Nannies CA strives to find the perfect nanny for your family within two weeks. If we currently have a nanny on staff that is not with a family we may be able to provide you with services sooner. If there are special qualifications that you would like your nanny to have it may take us slightly longer to provide you with a nanny.

What is your nanny screening process?

We strive to ensure that the nanny is safe, reliable, and trustworthy. To do this, Nannies CA first conducts a phone interview with several candidates. Next, we conduct in person interviews with the nanny that revolve around situations that they may encounter while interacting with your child / children. We also conduct criminal background checks, check for a valid driver's license, and ensure that each nanny has their CPR certification. We then conduct a background check on each nanny every three months that they are employed by us to further ensure your child's safety.

How does the nanny get paid?

The nanny is an employee of Nannies CA, so we will pay them.

Can I meet the nanny you have chosen prior to his/her hiring?

Yes, we can customize our search process to suit your needs. We can also arrange for you to meet with several candidates. We want you to feel 100% comfortable with your family's nanny.

What happens if I am not satisfied with my nanny?

If for any reason you are not satisfied with the nanny that we have provided, we ask that you notify us immediately and we will find you a replacement as quickly as possible for no additional charge.
