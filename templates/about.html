About Nannies CA

Nannies CA is a professional nanny service provider that works with families and nannies in the greater San Diego area. Some of the areas where we currently provide coverage are: San Diego, La Jolla, Carlsbad, Oceanside, Encinitas, Solana Beach, Del Mar, Escondido, Rancho Bernardo, Poway, and Chula Vista.

Our nannies have at least 2 years prior experience, background checks performed every three months, a valid and current driver's license, and their CPR certification.

Our Search Process

Your child's safety is our number one priority when looking for a nanny. For this reason, we put each candidate through a thorough screening process that involves multiple interviews and background checks.

  • We interview multiple candidates on the phone in order to gauge whether they will be a good fit for your family.
  • After narrowing down our search to the top three nannies, we will then perform face to face interviews with each remaining nanny.
  • At this time we also ensure that each nanny has a valid and current driver's license.
  • We rank the final candidates and perform thorough criminal background checks on each candidate so that we can provide you with the top ranked candidate that had a clean background check.
  • After deciding on the perfect nanny for your family we will then arrange for them to obtain their CPR certification if they do not already have it.
  • In order to ensure your child's continued safety, we also perform thorough background checks on each nanny every three months.

This entire process can be modified and specialized at your request.
