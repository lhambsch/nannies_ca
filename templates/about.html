About Nannies CA

Nannies CA is a high end nanny service provider that works with families and nannies in the greater San Diego area. Some of the areas where we currently provide coverage are: San Diego, La Jolla, Carlsbad, Oceanside, Encinitas, Solana Beach, Del Mar, Escondido, Rancho Bernardo, Poway, and Chula Vista.

Our nannies have at least 2 years prior experience, background checks performed every three months, a valid and current driver's license, and their CPR certification.
