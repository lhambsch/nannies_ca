About Nannies CA

Nannies CA is a professional nanny service provider that works with families and nannies in the greater San Diego area. Some of the areas where we currently provide coverage are: San Diego, La Jolla, Carlsbad, Oceanside, Encinitas, Solana Beach, Del Mar, Escondido, Rancho Bernardo, and Poway.

The childcare professionals we provide have at least 2 years prior experience, clean background checks, a valid and current driver's license, and their CPR certification.

Our Search Process

Your child's safety is our number one priority when looking for a someone to care for your child. For this reason, we put each candidate through a thorough screening process that involves multiple interviews and background checks.

  • We interview multiple candidates in the San Diego area on the phone in order to gauge whether they will be a good fit for your family.
  • After narrowing down our search to the top three nannies, we will then perform face to face interviews with each remaining candidate.
  • At this time we also ensure that each candidate has a valid and current driver's license.
  • We rank the final candidates and perform thorough criminal background checks on each candidate so that we can provide you with the top ranked candidate that had a clean background check.
  • After deciding on the perfect nanny for your family we will then arrange for them to obtain their CPR certification if they do not already have it.
  • In order to ensure your child's continued safety, we also perform thorough background checks on each of our nannies every three months.

This entire process can be modified and specialized at your request.
