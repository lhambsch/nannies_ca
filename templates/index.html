Find a nanny the easy way!

Are you a mom or dad that is heading back to work and looking for a trustworthy and experienced nanny to help with the kids? Or a busy parent that just needs a little break here and there? Are you exhausted when you get home from work and don't have time to cook dinner, do laundry, or clean the house? Are you and your spouse needing a date night by don't have anyone that you are comfortable with to watch your child? Do you just not have the time or energy to go through the interview and screening process to find a dependable nanny? Welcome to a world where all of these problems can be solved.

With Nannies CA, the process of finding a nanny becomes easy and stress-free. Providing you a nanny that is qualified, experienced, hardworking, trustworthy, and becomes part of your family is our expertise. Each nanny goes through an extensive interview process, and is subjected to a criminal background check in every state that they have resided in. Each nanny has a minimum of two years childcare experience, a current driver's license, auto insurance, at least 3 strong childcare references, and is CPR and first-aid certified. We want to get to know you and your family personally that's why we offer a free in-home consultation.

We understand making the decision to have a nanny come in to your home can be a little overwhelming, but we can help you through this process. Our goal is to make sure that your entire family is comfortable and satisfied throughout the entire process. If any part of our process does not fulfill your needs we will be happy to customize it for you.

How It Works

Nannies CA is a nanny agency that provides full-time, professional nanny services that are tailored to meet your needs. Our goal is to provide children with a nanny that will provide a caring and nurturing environment. We will replace your nanny if they do not meet your expectations for any reason.

Our Nannies

Nannies CA maintains strict requirements that each of our nannies must meet and maintain. All of our nannies have a minimum of two years of prior experience, background checks performed every three months, a valid and current driver's license, and a current CPR certification.

Nanny Guarantee

We believe in the importance of matching families with the correct nanny. If you are not satisfied with the nanny we have provided you within the first three months, we will replace them. No questions asked! No additional charge!
